OUR PRIMERS

PRIMERS

AL-611

Acrylic Primer

Acrylic Primer is a high-performance, water-based primer designed for both interior and exterior surfaces. It is formulated using premium acrylic polymers to ensure superior adhesion, durability, and protection for subsequent topcoats. This primer acts as a robust base layer, improving the bond between substrates and final coatings. It seals porous surfaces, ensuring a smooth, uniform finish while reducing paint absorption for enhanced coverage.

This primer is versatile and compatible with a variety of surfaces, including plaster, cement, masonry, drywall, and wood. Its low-VOC, non- toxic formula ensures an environmentally friendly application, offering excellent resistance to alkalis, efflorescence, and moisture ingress. Acrylic Primer dries quickly, enabling efficient project completion, and delivers exceptional opacity and whiteness, making it ideal for professional and DIY use.

By minimizing surface irregularities, Acrylic Primer contributes to longer-lasting, vibrant paint finishes, protecting substrates from environmental wear and tear. Its anti-fungal and anti-bacterial properties further enhance its durability, making it an excellent choice for a wide range of applications.

AD-629

Alkyd Primer

Alkyd Primer is a high-quality, solvent-based primer designed to provide excellent adhesion, durability, and protection for a variety of surfaces. Formulated with advanced alkyd resins, this primer serves as an ideal base for oil-based and solvent-based topcoats, enhancing their appearance and performance. Its superior sealing properties ensure reduced paint absorption, leading to better coverage and cost efficiency.

The primer is especially effective on wood, metal, and previously painted surfaces, providing excellent corrosion resistance and protection against moisture ingress. It also prevents tannin and stain bleed-through on wood and effectively blocks rust stains on metal substrates.

Alkyd Primer is easy to apply, offering a smooth, uniform finish with excellent sanding properties for a professional-grade final appearance. Its fast-drying formula allows for quick recoating, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor projects. It is available in a neutral white or gray shade to maximize topcoat opacity.

OB-638

Oil-Based Primer

Oil-Based Primer is a premium, solvent-based primer formulated to provide excellent adhesion, durability, and stain-blocking properties. Designed for both interior and exterior use, it creates a robust and reliable foundation for oil-based and water-based topcoats. The advanced formula penetrates and seals porous surfaces while blocking stains, such as tannins, watermarks, and smoke residue, ensuring a flawless finish. This primer is particularly effective on wood, metal, masonry, and other challenging surfaces, offering superior moisture resistance and preventing peeling or cracking of topcoats. It provides a smooth, sandable surface that enhances the longevity and appearance of the final paint layer.

Oil-Based Primer dries to a hard, durable finish, making it suitable for areas exposed to wear and tear. Its high opacity ensures excellent coverage, even on dark or uneven surfaces. Additionally, its formulation resists mildew, mold, and corrosion, making it ideal for humid or high- moisture environments.

PY-647

Polyurethane Primer

Polyurethane Primer is a premium-quality, two-component primer formulated with advanced polyurethane resins for maximum adhesion, durability, and chemical resistance. Engineered for superior performance, it acts as an excellent base layer for polyurethane and other high- performance coatings. This primer is designed for applications requiring exceptional resistance to abrasion, chemicals, and environmental exposure, making it ideal for industrial, commercial, and high-demand environments.

Its versatility allows for use on a wide range of substrates, including metal, wood, concrete, and previously coated surfaces. The primer enhances adhesion to these surfaces while sealing pores and imperfections, creating a smooth, uniform foundation for topcoats. It is particularly suited for environments exposed to moisture, UV radiation, or harsh chemicals.

Polyurethane Primer is fast-curing, reducing project timelines without compromising performance. Its high solids content provides excellent coverage and minimizes the need for multiple coats. With outstanding flexibility and weathering resistance, this primer ensures a long-lasting, protective base for topcoat systems.

ZC-656

PRO Zinc chromate Primer

PRO Zinc Chromate Primer is a high-performance, corrosion-resistant primer specifically designed to protect metal surfaces against rust and environmental degradation. Formulated with zinc chromate pigments, this solvent-based primer provides exceptional adhesion, durability, and a robust protective barrier against moisture and oxidation. Its anti-corrosive properties make it a preferred choice for ferrous and non- ferrous metal substrates.

This primer creates a smooth and uniform surface, ensuring optimal adhesion for subsequent topcoats while extending the lifespan of the coated system. With excellent flexibility and compatibility, Zinc Chromate Primer prevents chipping, cracking, and peeling under challenging environmental conditions.

Ideal for industrial, marine, automotive, and construction applications, it withstands harsh weather, UV exposure, and chemical contaminants. Zinc Chromate Primer is quick-drying and sandable, facilitating efficient application and time savings without compromising its protective qualities.

RO- 665

Red oxide Primer

Red Oxide Primer is a high-quality, anti-corrosive primer formulated with red oxide pigments and a durable resin base. This solvent-based primer provides exceptional adhesion and long-lasting protection for metal surfaces, shielding them from rust and environmental degradation. It penetrates and seals the substrate, creating a strong bond that enhances the performance and durability of subsequent topcoats.

The red oxide pigment offers superior coverage and visibility during application, ensuring consistent results. Its fast-drying formula allows for efficient application, reducing project timelines. Red Oxide Primer is designed for industrial, automotive, and structural metal applications, where robust corrosion resistance and durability are essential.

With excellent weather resistance, this primer performs well in both indoor and outdoor environments, even under harsh conditions. Its compatibility with various topcoats, including alkyd, acrylic, and polyurethane systems, makes it a versatile solution for diverse projects.

PRIMERS

SE-674

Self-Etching Primer

Self-Etching Primer is a high-performance primer specifically formulated to promote superior adhesion on bare metal surfaces. It combines a unique blend of acid-based components and resins to etch the surface chemically while providing a durable base coat. This dual-action primer not only adheres strongly to metal but also offers excellent corrosion resistance, making it an essential preparatory coating for professional and industrial applications.

This primer is ideal for hard-to-paint surfaces such as aluminum, galvanized steel, and stainless steel. Its formulation penetrates the substrate, creating a micro-etched surface that locks in the coating and ensures long-lasting performance. Self-Etching Primer is fast-drying, sandable, and compatible with various topcoat systems, including enamel, acrylic, and urethane paints.

Its versatility extends to automotive refinishing, construction projects, and industrial maintenance, where it acts as a reliable foundation for finishing coats. The product's anti-corrosive properties and compatibility with high-performance coatings make it suitable for use in harsh environments and outdoor applications.

PRIMERS

SL-683

Shellac Primer

Shellac Primer is a premium-quality, alcohol-based primer known for its excellent adhesion, stain-blocking capabilities, and versatility. Formulated with natural shellac resin dissolved in alcohol, this primer effectively seals porous surfaces and blocks tough stains such as water damage, nicotine, grease, tannins, and smoke residue. It is an ideal choice for preparing surfaces before painting, ensuring a smooth and professional finish.

Shellac Primer dries quickly, making it an excellent choice for projects requiring fast turnaround times. It adheres to a wide variety of substrates, including wood, drywall, plaster, metal, glass, and even glossy surfaces without the need for sanding. Additionally, its sealing properties prevent bleed-through of stains and odors, making it a preferred choice for renovation and restoration work.

The primer is compatible with both oil-based and latex topcoats, offering unmatched versatility. Its ability to lock out odors and cover stains makes it suitable for interior surfaces in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. Shellac Primer is available in various tintable shades to enhance topcoat color application.

WB-692

Water-Based Primer

Water-Based Primer is a high-performance, eco-friendly primer designed to enhance the adhesion and durability of topcoats while providing excellent coverage and surface preparation. Formulated with advanced waterborne resin technology, it delivers superior bonding to a variety of substrates, including drywall, wood, concrete, plaster, and masonry. Its low-VOC and odorless formula make it an ideal choice for interior applications, ensuring a safer and more comfortable working environment.

This primer is particularly effective in sealing porous surfaces, providing a smooth and uniform base for paint application. It dries quickly, reducing project turnaround times, and is compatible with a wide range of latex and acrylic topcoats. Water-Based Primer also features excellent stain-blocking properties, effectively covering minor stains, marks, and discolorations to ensure a flawless finish.

The non-flammable, user-friendly composition ensures safe handling, making it suitable for both professional painters and DIY enthusiasts. Additionally, the primer's ability to resist peeling, cracking, and chalking enhances the longevity and performance of subsequent coatings.
